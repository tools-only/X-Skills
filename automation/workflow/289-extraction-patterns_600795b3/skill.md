## Progress Tracking

### Signs of good extraction
- Expert shares specific examples
- Mental models are becoming clear
- Decision criteria are explicit
- Patterns are emerging
- Expert says "I never thought about it that way!"

### Signs of incomplete extraction
- Vague, abstract descriptions
- "It depends" without criteria
- Missing examples
- Gaps in workflow
- Expert seems uncertain

### When to probe deeper
- Expert uses jargon without defining
- Important points mentioned in passing
- Contradictions or inconsistencies
- Interesting but unexplored threads
- Decision points without criteria

### When to move on
- Point is thoroughly covered
- Multiple examples provided
- Principles are clear
- Expert confirms understanding
- Diminishing returns on more questions
