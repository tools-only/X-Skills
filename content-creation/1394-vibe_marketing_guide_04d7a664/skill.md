## What is Vibe Marketing?

**Vibe Marketing** is a plug-and-play system designed for creators, marketers, and founders who want to move fast and build momentum. It combines AI-powered workflows, Claude Code blueprints, and automation tools to:

- Simplify marketing strategy
- Automate content execution
- Scale content output while maintaining authenticity

**Core Philosophy**: Research → Strategy → Content → Revenue (in 1 hour instead of 2 weeks)

## The Research-to-Revenue Pipeline

### Phase 1: Site Analysis (15 min)
Use **Firecrawl MCP** to extract:
- Target Industry & ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Unique Value Propositions (UVPs)
- Brand Voice & Proof Points

**Output**: `Site-Exec-Summary.md`

### Phase 2: Competitive Intelligence (20 min)
Use **Perplexity MCP** to analyze:
- Search Intelligence & Keyword Trends
- Competitive Mapping
- Blue Ocean Opportunities

**Output**: `Market-Gap-Analysis.md`, `Digital-Presence-Audit.md`

### Phase 3: Content Strategy (15 min)
Synthesize findings into:
- Topic Gaps
- Format Gaps
- Positioning Gaps
- Question Gaps

**Output**: `Content-Gap-Analysis.md`

### Phase 4: Revenue Modeling (10 min)
Map gaps to revenue streams:
- Traffic Volume & Growth Rate
- Conversion Percentages
- Revenue Per User

**Output**: `Revenue-Projection.md`

### Phase 5: Content Creation
Draft publication-ready assets:
- First blog/video/newsletter
- Content calendar
- Cross-platform scripts

**Output**: `First-Post-Draft.md`, `Priority-Content-Plan.md`

## Core Templates

### 1. Site Executive Summary Template

```markdown
## Target Industry
[Your vertical and niche positioning]

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Core personas
- Pain points
- Motivations
- Buying triggers

## Unique Value Propositions (UVPs)
[Competitive differentiators linked to customer outcomes]

## Proof Points
[Metrics, testimonials, partnerships, media mentions]

## Brand Voice
- Tone
- Stylistic approach
- Do's and Don'ts
```

### 2. Content Gap Analysis Template

```markdown
## Topic Gaps
[Unaddressed subjects within your niche]

## Format Gaps
[Content styles competitors neglect]

## Positioning Gaps
[Tone/angle mismatches]

## Question Gaps
[High-search, low-answer topics]

## Prioritization Matrix
| Gap | Audience Demand | Competitive Weakness | ICP Alignment |
|-----|-----------------|---------------------|---------------|
```

### 3. Revenue Projection Template

```markdown
## Traffic Assumptions
- Monthly visitors: X
- Growth rate: Y%

## Conversion Funnel
- CTR: X%
- Conversion rate: Y%

## Revenue Streams
| Stream | Price | Volume | Monthly Revenue |
|--------|-------|--------|-----------------|

## Scenarios
| Scenario | Low | Expected | High |
|----------|-----|----------|------|
```

### 4. Influencer Patterns Template

```markdown
## Language Analysis
[Tone, phrasing, recurring vocabulary]

## Attention-Grabbing Openings
[Hooks, question-based openers]

## Repeatable Structures
[3-step protocols, comparison formats]

## Performance Metrics
| Pattern | Views | Engagement | Growth |
|---------|-------|------------|--------|
```

## Best Practices

### Do's
- Use templates as starting points, customize for your brand
- Feed all research into Claude for synthesis
- Update audits quarterly as positioning evolves
- Prioritize low-effort, high-return opportunities first

### Don'ts
- Don't skip competitive research
- Don't generate content without brand voice guidelines
- Don't ignore the Revenue Projection step
- Don't treat MCPs as magic - they're efficiency multipliers
