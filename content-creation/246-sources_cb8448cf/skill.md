## Visual Fundamentals

### Composition Theory
- **Rule of Thirds & Golden Ratio**: Classical photography and art composition principles, documented since Renaissance era
- **Leading Lines & Framing**: Standard cinematography education (ASC, BSC guidelines)
- **Visual Weight & Balance**: Art and design fundamentals (Dondis, "A Primer of Visual Literacy", 1973)

### Color Theory
- **Color Harmonies**: Johannes Itten, "The Art of Color" (1961)
- **Color Psychology**: Research aggregated from marketing and design studies
- **Film Color Grading**: Industry standard practices from colorists like Stefan Sonnenfeld, Bradford Young

### Lighting
- **Classic Portrait Lighting Patterns**: Rembrandt, butterfly, loop, split - Standard portrait photography education
- **Cinematography Lighting**: "Painting with Light" by John Alton (1949), ASC Master Class materials
- **Three-Point Lighting**: Standard film/video production technique

### Photography & Cinematography
- **Camera Movements**: ASC Manual, industry standard terminology
- **Lens Characteristics**: Physics of optics, documented in cinematography education
- **Film Stock Looks**: Kodak, Fuji official specifications and photographer documentation
