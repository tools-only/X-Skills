## How to Use This Checklist

### Verification Process:
1. **Locate Source**: Find primary or reliable secondary source
2. **Document Details**: Record full citation information
3. **Assess Quality**: Rate source reliability (A-D)
4. **Update Confidence**: Change ❓ or ⚠️ to ✅
5. **Cross-Reference**: Verify with multiple sources when possible

### Confidence Level Guide:
- ✅ **High Confidence**: Multiple reliable sources, well-documented
- ⚠️ **Medium Confidence**: Generally accepted but needs verification
- ❓ **Low Confidence**: Requires primary source documentation
